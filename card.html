Peregrine Falcon

The Peregrine falcon is widely know to be the fastest bird on Earth.

  • Scientific Name: Falco peregrinus
  • Average Length: 13 to 23 inches
  • Average Wingspan: 29 to 47 inches
  • Habitat: Coastlines, mountain ranges and cities

The Peregrine falcon is widely know for being the fastest bird on planet Earth. Peregrine falcons can dive and reach speeds of up to 242 mph1. The Peregrine falcon is also known to be an excellent hunter. Primarily feeding on other birds1.
